Earth

Welcome to planet Earth! Our home planet is third in line in terms of distance from the sun. This means earth sits far enough away that the sun did not just burn/blast away its atmosphere! Since earth's core is dense enough to spawn a strong magnetic field, This means the planet formed an atmosphere to repel or outright burn up foreign objects upon entry into said atmosphere. Earth is the only planet within the solar system that hosts liquid water(H2O). This unique aspect of earth's environment earns it it's nickname "The Blue Marble". Earth's atmosphere is 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen and 1% other elements. Since humans evolved on earth, this is the perfect combination of elements in terms of the composition of air we can breathe. If you want to learn more about earth, visit Nasa's website for further information.

Fun Facts!

  • Earth with it's gravitational compression taken into account is the most dense(heavy) planet in the solar system!
  • The blue marble/earth is the only planet within our solar system that harbors intelligent life.
  • Planet earth was once an uninhabitable ball of molten lava and constant tectonic activity early in its creation process!

Who Are We?

Welcome to Project Sol! The goal of the project is to educate anyone interested in our solar system. We have compiled information on many of the celestial bodies found within the Sol system. The entire purpose of this website is to convey the knowledge we have found to you! We hope that you will enjoy learning all about our solar system as you peruse the site. If you have any questions comments or concerns, please click the link at the top of the page that says contact us.
